Overview

Purpose

To position the company in the market, make a difference in terms of price, show the team of professionals who work with us, show the rivers we work in, and motivate people to go out into nature and try new things.

Audience

Who are our customers? Our customers have a high sense of adventure and interest in nature, they are young professionals who value outdoor experiences and are ready to explore new horizons. What do they want? They are lovers of emotion, so they are not afraid of risks. Our public wants intense, strong experiences, where they can be filled with adrenaline. What do they expect to find or what questions will they answer when accessing the website? They expect a good team and environment where they feel there is trust and that they can work together.
